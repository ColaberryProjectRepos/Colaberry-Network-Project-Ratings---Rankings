Colaberry is a company that focuses on data and uses data science to benchmark successful trainees and provides guided learning for trainees to succeed. Colaberry has gathered various objective and subjective data points over years from more than 2,500 trainees and more than 1,000,000 hours of accumulated individual training hours. 
Based on the data, one of key attributes that predict success is the consistency of the students completing their projects and loading them to the Colaberry network. A couple of data points are used to measure the consistency of students submitting their projects to the Colaberry network, and to measure the consistency of the students clicking through the completed projects and going through the steps by using the browse step or step by step options.
